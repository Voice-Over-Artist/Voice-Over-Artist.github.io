About

A narrator tuned for luminous storytelling

Bringing cinematic warmth and crystalline diction to scripts that deserve more than a "nice voice."

Layla Mansour is a professional voice artist specializing in luxury brand narration, documentaries, animation, and podcast imaging. Her reads balance velvet warmth with crystalline precision—ideal when projects need prestige without pretension, or when stories call for empathy anchored in clarity.

Working from an acoustically treated studio, Layla delivers polished, mastered files ready for broadcast and streaming. Directed sessions run via Zoom or Source-Connect, and every project is handled with meticulous attention to pacing, tone, and the specific demands of each brand's voice.
