About

A narrator tuned for luminous storytelling

Egyptian-Lebanese heritage, Anglo-Arabic fluency, and a microphone crafted for intimacy—bringing cinematic warmth to scripts that deserve more than a “nice voice.”

LM

Layla Mansour is a voice artist trusted by luxury houses and boutique studios alike. Her reads balance velvet tone with crystalline consonants—ideal when products need prestige without pomposity, or when documentaries call for empathy anchored in clarity.

Directed sessions run via Zoom, Source-Connect, or phone patch; unattended bookings arrive foldered, labeled, and mastered gently for streaming and broadcast loudness targets. Expect thoughtful pickups, timeline-aware pacing, and mixes that sit cleanly beside score and sound design.

  • Turnaround Typically 24–48 hours
  • Languages English · Arabic (MSA/Levantine)
