The studio

Where the magic happens

A fully treated recording space designed for broadcast-quality output—equipped for remote collaboration and quick turnaround at any hour.

Microphone & Signal Chain

Condenser microphone into a professional audio interface—clean, transparent gain staging that translates across monitors and earbuds alike.

DAW & Processing

Adobe Audition for editing, iZotope RX for surgical noise reduction, and loudness-compliant mastering to broadcast specs.

Acoustic Treatment

Professional absorption panels and bass traps yielding a noise floor well below broadcast thresholds—whisper-quiet even at high gain.

Remote Connectivity

Source-Connect, Zoom, and Google Meet for live-directed sessions. High-speed connection ensures stable, low-latency collaboration worldwide.

Turnaround & Delivery

Standard delivery within 24-48 hours. Rush turnaround available same-day. Files delivered as labeled WAV/MP3 stems ready for post.

Monitoring

Reference-grade monitoring for accurate playback—ensuring every take sounds exactly as intended before delivery.
