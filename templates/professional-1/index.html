About

Grounded reads with cinematic rhythm

Fifteen years behind the mic—guided listening, collaborative sessions, and mixes that translate cleanly across speakers, earbuds, and cinema chains.

AF

Amira Farouk is an Egyptian-American narrator known for articulate delivery and emotionally intelligent tone matching. With a broadcast engineering background and long-form narration experience, she keeps technical projects accurate while preserving warmth and momentum.

Sessions are directed live (Source-Connect, Zoom, or phone patch) or delivered as polished, labeled files ready for post. She regularly works across MENA brands, US docu-series, and global e-learning curricula.
