About

Professional voice, cinematic presence

Versatile narration that translates cleanly across speakers, earbuds, and cinema—always grounded, always human.

Amira Farouk is a professional voice-over artist and narrator with extensive experience across commercials, documentaries, e-learning, and audiobooks. Working from a fully treated studio with broadcast-standard equipment, she delivers polished reads that sit cleanly in any mix—from intimate whisper-close narration to confident retail energy.

Collaborative and direction-friendly, Amira works seamlessly with production teams via live sessions or delivers labeled, mastered files ready for post. Every project is handled with precision, quick turnaround, and a commitment to capturing exactly the right tone.
