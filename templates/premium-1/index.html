JH

James Harrington is a British-American voice artist trusted by agencies, studios, and streaming platforms for reads that sound effortless—yet survive ruthless mixes beside score, sfx, and competing VO lanes.

His career spans national television spots, prestige automotive launches, museum-grade documentaries, and character work where subtle shifts in rhythm reveal entire personalities. Directors cite his calm session presence, nimble script adaptation, and instinct for when to lean into velvet warmth versus crystalline clarity.

Sessions run remotely via broadcast-standard chains—clean gain staging, whisper-quiet booth, and mastered stems labeled for post teams who refuse surprises at final layback.
