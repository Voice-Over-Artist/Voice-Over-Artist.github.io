The studio

A broadcast-grade space built for precision

Every session originates from a purpose-built recording environment in Cairo—acoustically treated, electronically quiet, and equipped for remote collaboration at any hour.

Microphone & Signal Chain

Neumann TLM 103 into a Universal Audio Apollo interface—clean, transparent gain staging that translates across monitors and earbuds alike.

DAW & Processing

Adobe Audition and Pro Tools for editing, iZotope RX for surgical noise reduction, and loudness-compliant mastering to broadcast specs.

Acoustic Treatment

Floor-to-ceiling absorption panels, bass traps, and a floating floor design yielding a noise floor well below -60 dBFS—whisper-quiet even at high gain.

Remote Connectivity

Source-Connect, Zoom, and Google Meet for live-directed sessions. High-speed fiber ensures stable, low-latency connections worldwide.

Turnaround & Delivery

Standard delivery within 24–48 hours. Rush turnaround available same-day. Files delivered as labeled WAV/MP3 stems ready for post.

Location

Based in Cairo, Egypt—serving clients across the Middle East, Europe, and North America with flexible scheduling across time zones.
